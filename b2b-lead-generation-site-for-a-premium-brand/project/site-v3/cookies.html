What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data your browser stores on behalf of a site. They let a site remember your preferences (like the language and brand mode you chose) and let us understand how the site is used in aggregate.

What we use

Essential. A small set of values stored locally to remember your language, your selected brand mode (calm or bold), and your cookie choice itself. These are not shared with anyone and cannot be turned off without breaking these features.

Analytics (optional). When you accept analytics cookies, we load Google Analytics 4 (GA4) which sets cookies in your browser to count visits, understand which content is most read, and measure whether visitors complete the inquiry form. We do not use this data to identify you personally and we do not sell it.

Third parties

  • Google Analytics 4 — only when you explicitly accept analytics.
  • WhatsApp — used only when you click a "Talk to us" link, which opens WhatsApp directly. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies once you arrive there.

Manage your choice

Use the "Cookie preferences" link in the site footer to reopen the consent banner at any time and change your selection.
